board-fitted enclosure generator

Board
Measure your own board: enter its size, then add holes and ports under Connectors.
Fit and height
Raise this for a shield, HAT, or a tall cooler.
Walls and material
Lid window / display
A cutout in the lid for a display, an LED matrix, or a window. Position is from the lid centre.
Recess pocket: a blind rectangular recess milled into the lid top with the CSG engine (for a label or an inset). Set width to 0 for none.
Lid text
Wall text
A raised legend on the outside of a wall. Recessed wall text needs boolean carving and is not in this version.
Cooling and access
Case mounting
The DIN cradle snaps on with a flex. The sprung latch uses thinner lips with a snap lead-in; print in PETG or ABS and tune with the coupon.
Needs a thicker wall to leave solid lips either side of the channel. Skipped with a warning if the wall is too thin. A matching bead is added to the lid underside to seat into the channel.
Cable entry
Round holes sized for cable glands, with an optional zip-tie strain-relief saddle just inside the wall. Offset is from the centre of the wall.
Connectors
Toggle a port on or off, and edit its offset along the wall, its height above the PCB, and its cutout size. Values are typical starting points; measure your board and adjust, then save the project. Offset is measured from the centre of the wall.
Custom board: add your own ports and mounting holes. Saved with the project.

SCABBARD

    
Field Instrument FI-111, version 2.0.0. A sheath fitted to a blade:
       give it a board and it generates a printable enclosure sized to that board, with mounting bosses,
       connector cutouts, a choice of lid, and text.

    
Everything runs on your machine, straight from disk. No server, no account, no upload. Geometry, STL
       and 3MF export, the ZIP writer, mounting hardware, gasket channel, a hand-rolled boolean CSG engine,
       and the preview are all hand-rolled in one file.

Credits

    
Triangulation uses the ear-clipping approach popularized by Mapbox earcut (ISC), hand-ported and
       adapted for watertight extrusion. STL, meshing, and the isometric renderer are original.

    
Feedback

    
Found a board that does not fit, or a dimension that is off? Send it along via
       greenshoegarage.com.

    
Asset budget: zero external assets. No web fonts, no images, no network.
       Print settings are your slicer's job, this is not a slicer.
